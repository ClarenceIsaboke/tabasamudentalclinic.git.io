About Tabasamu Dental Clinic

Hey you, welcome to our Clinic. You can bet we are excited to have you here. Yes, though unfortunate, any concern regarding the health of your teeth is our joy, why? that's because we are here to fix them. Did you know that our dental clinic is the most prefferred dental clinic, across the Nairobi region as vetted by Ipson Synovate inconjuction with The Dental Association of Kenya? well, now you know. Wink! We are registered under the Kenya Dental Practitioneers Association and the Dentists Board act(056A). We opened our doors for our esteemed clients from the 5th of March 2015, with an aim to treat and teach dental health to everyone who has teeth. We are a team of young intelligent doctors from across the globe passionate about the health of your teeth. We offer a wide range of general and specialized dental services including restorative dentistry, gum treatment and cosmetic dental services.

Dental x-ray

In the privacy of your home, you can now whiten your teeth using a Take-Home Whitening Kit. The new formula has enamel protection and a better flavor. All that is required is an appointment with the dentist so impressions of your teeth can be taken to create two plastic trays, which are used to apply the bleaching gel on your teeth. These trays are worn according to a program designed specifically for your lifestyle, as you can either choose to wear during the day or at night. You will have a dazzling white smile in 10 to 14 days.
